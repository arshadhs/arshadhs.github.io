# Differentiation of Univariate Functions

Differentiation measures rate of change.

For a function f(x), the derivative measures the rate of change.

{{% colour RED %}}
$[
f'(x) = $lim_{h $to 0} $frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}
$]
{{% /colour %}}

Interpretation:
- Slope of tangent
- Instantaneous rate of change
